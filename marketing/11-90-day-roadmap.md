## North-star metrics
- **Supply:** verified advertisers, listings live, multi-city profiles.
- **Demand:** organic sessions, city-page impressions/clicks (GSC), AI citations.
- **Loop:** referral signups, artifact shares.
- **Revenue:** token purchases, Featured/Slider conversions.
- **Channel efficiency:** advertiser CAC by source (you have attribution).

## The one sentence to remember
You're not short on product. You're short on distribution — and in a vertical
locked out of paid ads, owned + earned channels (SEO, AEO, newsletter, content,
tools, referral) *are* the business. Pick two, start this week.
