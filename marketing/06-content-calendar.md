# 06 — Content Calendar + Repurposing Engine

The video's Strategy 7: record **one** pillar piece a week, repurpose into
50+ assets. This is how a small team out-publishes everyone in the niche.

> Channel reality for adult: mainstream platforms (Meta, TikTok, Google) restrict
> explicit adult content and ads. So the content engine leans on: your own
> blog/SEO, X/Twitter (more permissive), Reddit (relevant subreddits, within
> rules), niche adult forums and directories, your newsletter, and adult-friendly
> social (e.g. specialized networks). Keep everything tasteful/SFW-marketing in
> tone to survive on as many channels as possible.

## The weekly pillar → many pattern

1. **Record one 30-min pillar** (voice memo or short video). Topics rotate
   between three buckets:
   - **Trust/safety** (client-facing, SFW, highly shareable): "How to verify
     an escort is real", "Staying safe meeting someone new", "What discreet
     actually means".
   - **Advertiser growth** (supply-facing): "How to write a profile that gets
     booked", "Free vs Featured: what actually earns more", "Multi-city reach".
   - **City/market** (SEO + local): "A discreet guide to {city}", "Where demand
     is highest in {country}".
2. **Transcribe** (you have `/watch` locally, or Whisper/Otter).
3. **Repurpose with Claude/`/market social`** into:
   - 1 blog post (→ feeds SEO + AEO; cross-link city pages)
   - 1 newsletter issue
   - 5–10 X/Twitter posts (1 thread + standalone tips)
   - 3–5 Reddit/forum posts (value-first, link sparingly, follow each sub's rules)
   - 5–10 quote graphics (pull-quotes from the pillar, branded subtly)
   - 2–3 short clips (if video)
4. **Schedule** across the week.

Repeat the structure, rotating cities (Antwerp, Amsterdam, Berlin, Paris…) and
themes. After 3 months you'll have ~12 pillars → 150+ assets and a real content
moat.

## Anti-slop rules (from the video)
- Keep a brand guide + reference images so visuals are consistent, not generic
  (see `10-brand-voice.md`).
- Edit AI output — never publish raw. The value is in the repurposing *plus* a
  human pass.
- Lead with genuine value (safety, how-to), not "buy now". Earn the audience.

## Distribution channels ranked for this vertical
1. **Own blog / SEO** — safest, compounding, you control it.
2. **Newsletter** — owned, can't be deplatformed (see `07`).
3. **X/Twitter** — most permissive mainstream platform; build in public.
4. **Reddit + niche forums** — high-intent, but value-first and rule-aware.
5. **Adult-friendly directories/partners** — your `/partners` page network.
6. **Quote graphics / shareable artifacts** — see `09`.

## Tooling
- `/market social {topic}` — generate the social calendar from a pillar.
- `/market copy` — sharpen any individual post.
- `/watch` (local) — transcribe video pillars.
