# 10 — Brand Voice Guide

So every email, page, post, and artifact sounds like one confident brand — and
so AI-generated content gets edited toward a consistent voice (anti-slop).

## Positioning in one line
The **safe, verified, discreet** place to advertise and discover adult
companionship in the EU — an advertising platform, not a booking middleman.

## Voice: three words
**Discreet. Confident. Warm.**

- **Discreet** — privacy is the product. Never crude, never explicit in
  marketing copy. Suggest, don't spell out. "An evening that ends behind a closed
  door," not graphic detail.
- **Confident** — premium, calm, sure of itself. Short declarative sentences.
  No hype, no exclamation-mark spam, no "amazing!!!". The quality speaks.
- **Warm** — human and respectful to both advertisers (your customers) and
  clients. Advertisers are professionals running a business; treat them like it.

## Tone by audience
| Audience | Lean |
|---|---|
| Advertisers | Empowering, business-like: "keep 100%", "you decide", "go live today" |
| Clients | Reassuring, tasteful: "verified", "discreet", "browse with confidence" |
| Processors/partners/press | Precise, compliance-forward: "advertising platform", "verified", "GDPR" |

## Do
- "Advertiser", "advertisement", "listing", "booking request", "verified",
  "discreet", "private", "independent".
- Lead with trust and privacy.
- Use real numbers when you have them; conservative estimates when you don't.
- Em-dash sparingly; prefer clean sentences.

## Don't
- Don't use crude/explicit language in marketing surfaces.
- Don't say "buy", "purchase", or imply paying for the *service* on-platform —
  it's always "advertising credits"/"tokens" and "booking *requests*". (This is
  brand *and* compliance.)
- Don't overpromise earnings or traffic.
- Don't use generic AI filler ("in today's fast-paced world", "unlock the power
  of", "elevate your experience", "delve"). Cut it.

## Visual identity (from the codebase)
- **Colors:** near-black backgrounds (`#050505`/`#0e0e0e`), gold accent
  (`#c5a05a`), warm off-white text (`#ece8e1`).
- **Type:** Cormorant Garamond (serif headings, often italic for elegance) +
  Poppins/Poppins-sans body.
- **Feel:** dark, gold-accented, premium, restrained. Lots of space. Subtle
  glows, not loud gradients.
- **Logo in artifacts:** subtle but present (so people share them).

## Tagline bank
- "An evening that ends behind a closed door." (existing — atmospheric)
- "Discreet, verified, premium — across Europe."
- "Verified advertisers. Total discretion."
- Advertiser-side: "List free. Keep everything. Stay private."

## Anti-slop checklist for any AI-drafted content
- [ ] Sounds like the three words above?
- [ ] No crude language, no "buy the service" implication?
- [ ] Filler phrases cut?
- [ ] Numbers honest?
- [ ] A human edited it before publishing?
