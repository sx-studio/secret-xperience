# Payment Processor Outreach

Reusable enquiry email for high-risk / escort-listing merchant account brokers.

## Who to send to (send SEPARATELY, one provider per email)

Realistic channel is **brokers/ISOs** that place you with an acquiring bank — not
direct adult PSPs (Verotel = escort-only sites only; CCBill = declines escorts).

- **QuadraPay** — explicitly handles escort *listing* sites, EU/SEPA acquiring (1–2 day settlement). Most concrete EU fit. https://quadrapay.com/escort-merchant-account/
- **Instabill** — advertises escort accounts, but mixed real-world results (some report advertised-then-declined). Treat as a maybe. https://instabill.com/ecommerce-industries/escort-merchant-accounts/
- **Merchant Advice Service** (UK broker, covers EU). https://www.merchantadviceservice.co.uk/high-risk-merchant-accounts/escort-merchant-accounts

Tips:
- Do NOT put all three in one To/CC — processors dislike being shopped against named competitors.
- Fill the volume line honestly. Pre-revenue = "early-stage, launching." Overstating backfires at underwriting.
- Always confirm escort-listing acceptance BEFORE sharing KYC docs (avoids the
  advertised-then-declined runaround).

## Email template

**Subject:** Escort listing/advertising platform — merchant account enquiry (EU, B2B advertising model)

Hello,

I run **SecretXperience** (secretxperience.eu), a premium adult-services advertising marketplace operating in the EU (primary markets: Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, France, Luxembourg). I'm looking for a card-processing / merchant-account solution and want to confirm fit before sharing documentation.

**Important up front:** I'd appreciate a clear yes/no on whether you can place a business of this type **before** we exchange KYC documents. I've seen providers advertise escort support and then decline after collecting sensitive paperwork, so I'd rather establish fit early.

**What we are (and aren't):**
- We are an **advertising platform**. Advertisers (independent providers) purchase on-site credits ("tokens") to post and promote listings. **This is the only thing we charge for — B2B advertising fees.**
- **No payment for any escort/companionship service passes through our platform.** Those categories are strictly meetup-request only; there is no on-platform booking or card payment for the service itself.
- So the card transactions you'd process are **advertisers buying advertising credit** — not adult-service bookings.

**Compliance posture:**
- **Mandatory ID verification** for every advertiser before they can publish (government ID + selfie review, with explicit 18+/consent confirmation recorded).
- Age and ownership consent captured at submission.
- Operating within EU legal frameworks; [legal entity name], incorporated in [country].
- Existing content moderation and listing-approval workflow before anything goes live.

**What I'm asking:**
1. Do you accept **escort listing / adult advertising platforms** of this structure? (Yes/No)
2. Do you support **EU acquiring with SEPA settlement** to a European business bank account?
3. What are your indicative **rates, rolling reserve, and high-risk setup fees**?
4. What **documentation** do you need to proceed, and typical **approval timeline**?
5. Any **monthly volume minimums**? (We are [early-stage / processing approx. €X per month and growing].)

Happy to provide full incorporation and compliance documentation once we've confirmed mutual fit.

Thank you,
[Your name]
support@secretxperience.eu · secretxperience.eu
